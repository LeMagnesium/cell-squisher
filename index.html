The game is about vengance, on the vicious cells rebelling in the Petri dish. Your score is important, keep an eye on it.

When a cell is squished by your scientific wrath (in the form of your 7 digits smasher hand), it will become dark and die,

offering all its health to the powers of science. When a cell manages to escape your anger, it robs 10 points from your

score along the way! When you squish a cell as another one is dying, you increase your combo. As long as you squish cells

while others are still dying, your combo increases, to fall back to 0 when no cell is dying. Overlapping cells offer a

bonus of 50 points for each cell after the first one.

Now go! THOSE CELLS SHALL FEEL THE WRAATH OF SCIEENCE!

~*~ Changelog ~*~
  • It is now possible to skip to the next tune from the audio menu, though it is a random one, if you keep the random play mode on. You can alternate between the random and loop play modes using another button in the audio menu : the play mode button. Two other buttons let you increase and decrease the volume level by 5%, and the last one, mute the tune. Note : the tunes will still be "played", but muted. If you enable muting, the previous volume level is saved and set back once unmuted. If you lower the level to 0% using the minus button, and unmute, you'll be back at 5%.
  • Upon building a combo, a path will be drawn shoving the last 50 (or less) cells you've squished. There will be soon an option to disable it for performance enhancement.
  • Reaching 50 of combo will trigger a slight shaking. Each stage of 50 will increase the shakiness.
